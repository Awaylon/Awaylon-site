About Us

Brand Story

Brand Story

Awaylon is a brand specializing in the R&D, production, and sales of air conditioner diffusers. We are located in Ningde City, Fujian Province, China, with years of industry experience and technical expertise.

From the beginning, we have been committed to breaking the monotonous design of traditional air conditioner diffusers, combining artistic aesthetics with practical functionality. Our products use eco-friendly PVC+ABS materials, carefully designed and crafted to make every diffuser a highlight of your space.

Over the years, we have always adhered to the business philosophy of "Quality First, Customer First", providing high-quality products and services for numerous families and engineering projects, earning widespread recognition and praise.

Design Philosophy

Design Philosophy

Minimalist Aesthetics · Invisible Comfort · Eco-Friendly Health

Minimalist Aesthetics

We pursue minimalist design philosophy, removing cumbersome decorations and letting products present in their purest form, perfectly integrated with various decoration styles.

Invisible Comfort

Invisible frameless design allows the diffuser to perfectly blend with the wall without affecting overall aesthetics, while ensuring good ventilation.

Eco-Friendly Health

Using high-quality PVC+ABS eco-friendly materials, odorless and free from harmful substances, ensuring you and your family breathe healthier air.

Exquisite Quality

Every product undergoes strict quality inspection with fine craftsmanship and durable construction, providing reliable product guarantee.
