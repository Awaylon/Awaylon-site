FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How is the product price calculated?

A: Product price is determined based on product model, size, quantity, and customization requirements. You can tell us your specific needs through the inquiry form and we will provide you with a detailed quote.

Q: What customization methods do you support?

A: We support size customization, color customization, style customization, and custom-from-image. You just need to provide specific requirements or drawings, and we can produce for you.

Q: What is the installation method for your products?

A: Our products use ceiling-mounted installation, suitable for various central air and fresh air systems. P50S series supports gypsum board recessed installation. Please consult a professional if you have any questions.

Q: What is the delivery time?

A: Standard products usually ship within 3-5 working days, customized products delivery time depends on specific requirements. We will arrange production and shipment for you as soon as possible.
